# DIWALI-SALES-ANALYSIS-
DIWALI SALES ANALYSIS USING PYTHON AN POWERBI

“This project demonstrates my ability to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights using Power BI.”

Project Overview

This project analyses sales data to understand customer behaviour, product performance, and regional trends using Power BI.

The dashboard provides insights into:

Sales distribution
Customer demographics
Product category performance
Regional sales trends

📄 Project Reference:

🎯 Objective
Analyse total sales performance
Identify high-performing product categories
Understand customer segmentation by age and gender
Evaluate regional sales distribution
📊 Key Metrics
💰 Total Sales: 106.25M
👉 Indicates overall business performance and revenue scale
🔍 Key Insights & Interpretation
1. 👥 Customer Demographics (Age & Gender)

📄 (Page 1 & 3 visuals)

Age group 26–35 contributes highest sales
Followed by 36–45 and 18–25

👉 Interpretation:

Young and mid-age customers are the primary revenue drivers

Gender split:
~70% Female
~30% Male

👉 Insight:

Female customers contribute significantly more to total sales

2. 🛍 Product Category Performance

📄 (Page 1 & 3 pie/donut charts)

Top categories:

Clothing & Apparel
Food
Electronics

👉 Interpretation:

These categories generate the majority of revenue and should be prioritized

Low-performing categories:

Tupperware
Stationery
Pet Care

👉 Insight:

These categories may need marketing or pricing strategy improvements

3. 💼 Sales by Occupation

📄 (Page 1 bar chart)

Top contributing sectors:

IT Sector
Healthcare
Aviation

👉 Interpretation:

Working professionals, especially in IT and Healthcare, are key customers

4. 🌍 Regional Sales Analysis

📄 (Page 2 & 4 map visuals)

Higher sales observed in:
Western and Southern regions
Major cities like Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad

👉 Interpretation:

Urban and economically developed regions drive higher sales

5. 📈 Age Group Spending Behaviour

📄 (Page 3 bar chart)

Peak spending: 26–35 age group
Lowest: 0–17 and 55+

👉 Insight:

Middle-aged consumers have higher purchasing power

🔥 Key Business Insights
Female customers are the primary revenue contributors
Young professionals (26–35) drive maximum sales
Clothing, Food, and Electronics dominate revenue
Urban regions contribute the most to sales
Certain product categories have low demand and need attention
🛠 Tools Used
Power BI
Data Visualization
DAX
🚀 Conclusion

This dashboard highlights how customer demographics and product categories influence sales performance. Businesses can use these insights to:

Target high-value customer segments
Optimize product strategies
Focus on high-performing regions
